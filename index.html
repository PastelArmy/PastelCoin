🎯 About

PastelCoin ($NATA) is a community-driven memecoin born in Portugal — inspired by the iconic pastel de nata and powered by culture, humour and blockchain.

Our mission is simple and sweet: allow people to use $NATA to buy pastel de nata in pastelarias across the world.

Built on the BNB Chain, PastelCoin is fully transparent, with no presale, no team allocation, and no hidden taxes.

$NATA is for pastry lovers, meme dreamers, nata maximalists and crypto degenerates who believe web3 should taste better.
